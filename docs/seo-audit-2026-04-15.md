## Issues by Priority

### CRITICAL (3 issues)

| # | Issue | Impact | Fix |
|---|-------|--------|-----|
| 1 | **Cloudflare Managed AI Bot Block** overrides custom Allow rules | GPTBot, ClaudeBot, Google-Extended cannot crawl the site | Cloudflare Dashboard > Security > Bots > AI Bots > disable managed block |
| 2 | **Course schema duplicated on 18+ pages** | Google may flag as spammy structured data | `BaseLayout.astro:35` — change default `showCourseSchema = true` to `false`. Set `true` only in `index.astro`. |
| 3 | **BreadcrumbList broken on all lesson pages + secondary pages** | 1-item breadcrumbs are semantically useless | Pass breadcrumb data from `LessonLayout.astro`, or set `showBreadcrumb={false}` on pages without proper depth |

| 6 | **Duplicate meta keywords** across blog posts | Identical keywords signal low-effort SEO | Make keywords dynamic from post data in `[slug].astro:84` |
| 7 | **Lesson meta descriptions too short** (82-100 chars vs 150 target) | May get replaced by Google-generated snippets | Expand lesson MDX frontmatter descriptions |
