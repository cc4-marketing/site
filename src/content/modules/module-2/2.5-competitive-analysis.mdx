## Strategic Competitive Intelligence

Understanding your competitive landscape is crucial for effective positioning and messaging. Claude Code can help you research competitors, analyze their strategies, and identify opportunities.

### What You'll Learn

- Comprehensive competitor analysis
- Positioning and messaging analysis
- Competitive content strategy research
- Feature and pricing comparison
- Identifying competitive advantages

## Comprehensive Competitor Profile

```
Create detailed competitor profile for RescueTime:

Research and document:

COMPANY OVERVIEW:
- Company size, funding, history
- Target market and positioning
- Business model and pricing
- Key metrics (if public)

PRODUCT ANALYSIS:
- Core features and capabilities
- Unique selling propositions
- Product strengths and weaknesses
- Roadmap and recent updates

MARKETING ANALYSIS:
- Brand positioning and messaging
- Target audience and personas
- Key marketing channels
- Content strategy and topics
- Social media presence
- Paid advertising approach

STRENGTHS VS FOCUSFLOW:
- What they do better
- Features we lack
- Marketing advantages
- Brand recognition

WEAKNESSES VS FOCUSFLOW:
- Gaps we can exploit
- Features they lack (especially team features)
- Marketing vulnerabilities
- Customer complaints and pain points

OPPORTUNITIES FOR US:
- Positioning angles to emphasize
- Messaging that differentiates
- Features to highlight in comparison
- Market segments they underserve

Save as research/competitors/rescuetime-full-profile.md
```

## Competitive Positioning Matrix

```
Create positioning analysis for productivity tools market:

Analyze 5 main competitors:
- RescueTime (individual resource allocation)
- Freedom (distraction blocking)
- Focus@Will (music for focus)
- Centered (flow state app)
- Forest (gamified focus)

For each, identify:

POSITIONING AXES:
- Individual vs Team focus (X-axis)
- Passive tracking vs Active intervention (Y-axis)

POSITIONING STATEMENT:
- Who they serve
- What problem they solve
- How they solve it uniquely

MESSAGING THEMES:
- Primary value propositions
- Emotional vs rational appeal
- Key differentiators emphasized

MARKET POSITION:
- Price point (budget/mid/premium)
- Target segment (individuals/teams/enterprise)
- Brand perception (scientific/fun/serious/etc.)

OUR POSITIONING:
- Where Planerio fits in the landscape
- White space we can own
- Positioning statement refinement
- Competitive moats we're building

Create visual positioning map description
Save as research/competitive-positioning-matrix.md
```

## Win/Loss Analysis

```

When we win deals:

WHY WE WON:

- Pricing advantages
- Brand perception
- Sales process effectiveness
- Customer service factors

COMPETITIVE WEAKNESSES EXPOSED:
- What customers didn't like about competitors
- Missing features that mattered
- Pricing concerns
- Support issues

When we lose deals:

WHY WE LOST:
- Features we lacked
- Pricing disadvantages
- Brand recognition gaps
- Integration limitations
- Trust/credibility issues

COMPETITOR STRENGTHS:
- What they did better
- Features that swayed decisions
- Brand advantage

LEARNINGS:
- Product roadmap priorities
- Messaging improvements
- Sales objection handling
- Marketing emphasis areas

Create template for ongoing tracking
Save as research/win-loss-analysis-template.md
```

```

- Immediate response plan
- Messaging to existing customers
- Sales team talking points
- Marketing counter-positioning
- Product differentiation emphasis

SCENARIO 2: Competitor drops pricing
- Pricing strategy review
- Value-based messaging reinforcement
- Premium positioning justification
- Promotion/offer considerations

- Response guidelines
- Fact-checking process
- When to engage vs ignore
- PR/communications protocol

SCENARIO 4: Competitor wins major customer
- Case study response
- Competitive win story creation
- Sales enablement updates
- Positioning refinement

SCENARIO 5: New competitor enters market
- Assessment criteria
- Threat level evaluation
- Response priority
- Intelligence gathering plan

For each scenario:
- Decision criteria
- Response timeline
- Stakeholders to involve
- Communication templates
- Success metrics

Save as campaigns/competitive-response-playbook.md
```
